# Info

The info page is for unauthenticated users to learn about Gotcha.

**The page has 2 sections:**
- **What is Gotcha?** — explains the game concept: social elimination game, secret targets, eliminating targets by tagging them, taking their target after a successful kill, last one standing wins.
- **What this app offers** — explains why the app exists: removes the hassle of manual setup on paper, lets the organiser invite players via a shareable link, tracks kills automatically.
